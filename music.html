my original music

as

More experimental music, often written on piano/keys. They tend to show my more electronic influences. So far, mostly instrumentals which aim to tell a non-verbal story and be constantly evolving texturally.

with

Off-the-cuff rock music, often beginning with drums or a guitar riff. Notes of garage rock, punk, grunge, metal, bluesy rock, and similar genres. Mostly improvised. More impulsive and loud. I play all instruments, write, sing, record, and produce our music myself.

partridge inquiry

as

A collection of experiments not fit for my other pages. Long-form textures and atmospheres created for my own amusement, but shared for anyone who may be curious. Typically created by running cheap keyboards, samples of my other recordings, mundane sounds, voice memos, various microphones, etc. into various mixing boards, manipulating them in real time as a performance, sometimes tweaking things further on the computer afterward.
